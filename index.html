Who is

The Artist

Feeling Gaga is a Guwahati-based DJ and turntablist carving a bold presence in North East India's growing music scene. Blending the raw energy of Hip Hop and Turntablism with the infectious groove of Bollywood, Punjabi and multi-genre open format sets, Feeling Gaga delivers performances that are both technically sharp and emotionally charged.

From commanding the decks at Kharkaa Disco to rocking college crowds at SVEA and creating unforgettable moments at private events, Feeling Gaga brings the same dedication and energy to every room — big or small. Rooted in the craft of real DJing with professional Pioneer turntables and mixer, every set is a live, hands-on, authentic performance.

With a growing social media presence at @feeling.gaga and a sound that connects across generations, Feeling Gaga is a name to watch in North East India's nightlife and events circuit.
